Macros and Limits

Macros for math limits.

Description

The macro names given in the following list must use the values specified. These constant expressions are suitable for use in #if preprocessing directives.

#define FLT_DIG

6

#define FLT_MANT_DIG

24

#define FLT_MAX_10_EXP

+38

#define FLT_MAX_EXP

+128

#define FLT_MIN_10_EXP

-37

#define FLT_MIN_EXP

-125

#define FLT_RADIX

2

#define FLT_MAX

0x1.fffffep127f

#define FLT_MIN

0x1.0p-126f

#define FLT_EPSILON

0x1.0p-23f
