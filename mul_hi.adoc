[[mul_hi]]
= mul_hi

Returns the high half of the product of `x` and `y`.

[source,c]
----
gentype mul_hi(gentype x,
               gentype y)
----


[[description]]
== Description

`mul_hi` computes `x` * `y` and returns the high half of the product of `x` and `y`.
